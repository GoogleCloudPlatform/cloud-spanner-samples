Explanation

This is an explanation of how the system works

We have created a Spanner Graph Database to store the stations in the London Underground and pre-calculated all of the transit times between the stations.

In addtion we have Oyster transit cards, mapped to riders and the physical addresses that they are mapped to.

In our Tube system riders to not need to swipe out, but they need to swipe in.

We collect every swipe and store it in the database. We then check to see if the card has been cloned, by determining the shortest travel times between stations and flag a card if it gets somewhere faster than is possible
